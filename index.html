Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BMI Calculator ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

Information Collection and Use

BMI Calculator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter (height, weight, age, gender, name) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (SharedPreferences) and never leaves your device.

No Account Required

The app does not require any account creation, registration, or login. You can use all features immediately after installation.

No Internet Access

This version of BMI Calculator does not require any internet permissions. The app functions entirely offline with no network access.

Data Storage

  • All BMI records, personal profiles, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Data is persisted using Android SharedPreferences.
  • No data is uploaded to any server or cloud service.
  • You can delete all data at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The app may be used to track BMI for children under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. All data remains on the device and is not accessible to us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after being posted.
